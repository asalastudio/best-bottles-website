\# About Best Bottles

Best Bottles is a premium perfume bottle supplier specializing in luxury glass vessels for fragrance brands. We source the finest bottles from artisan glassmakers worldwide and offer custom design services, making premium packaging accessible to brands of all sizes.

\#\# Our Story

Best Bottles was founded on a simple belief: \*\*the bottle matters as much as what's inside\*\*. We saw too many exceptional fragrances trapped in generic packaging, and we knew there was a better way.

We built relationships with the world's finest glassmakers and bottle producers to curate a collection of premium vessels. Whether you need stock bottles delivered quickly or custom designs developed specifically for your brand, we make luxury packaging accessible.

We believe that a perfume bottle is more than packaging \- it's the first touch point between a customer and a fragrance. It sets expectations, communicates value, and becomes a treasured object long after the perfume is gone.

\#\# Our Mission

To elevate the packaging experience by providing \*\*"Muted Luxury"\*\* solutions that blend timeless artisanal craftsmanship with modern sustainable practices.

\# Visual Identity & Premium Image Direction

\#\# Brand Aesthetic: "Muted Luxury"  
Sophisticated, understated, and timeless. All generated content and imagery should adhere to this aesthetic.

\#\# Color Palette

\#\#\# Primary Colors  
\- \*\*Off-Black/Charcoal\*\* (\`\#1D1D1F\`): Use for primary text, headers, and strong accents. Conveys solidity and sophistication.  
\- \*\*Warm Off-White/Bone\*\* (\`\#F5F3EF\`): Use for backgrounds to create a soft, inviting canvas. Avoid harsh pure white (\`\#FFFFFF\`).

\#\#\# Accent Colors  
\- \*\*Muted Gold/Bronze\*\* (\`\#C5A065\`): Use sparingly for buttons, highlights, and icons. Should look metallic and refined, not yellow or gaudy.  
\- \*\*Slate Blue/Grey\*\* (\`\#637588\`): Use for secondary text and subtle borders.  
\- \*\*Warm Beige\*\* (\`\#EBE7DD\`): Use for section dividers and card backgrounds.

\#\# Typography

\#\#\# Headings  
\- \*\*Font Style:\*\* Serif (similar to 'Playfair Display' or 'Bodoni')  
\- \*\*Characteristics:\*\* Elegant, high-contrast strokes  
\- \*\*Usage:\*\* Use for impact and luxury feel  
\- \*\*Styling:\*\* Uppercase with wide letter spacing (tracking) for labels and small headers

\#\#\# Body Text  
\- \*\*Font Style:\*\* Sans-serif (similar to 'Inter', 'Roboto', or 'Lato')  
\- \*\*Characteristics:\*\* Clean, legible, and modern  
\- \*\*Usage:\*\* All body copy, descriptions, technical specs

\#\# Imagery Style (Premium Direction)

\#\#\# Subject Matter  
\- Glass bottles, vials, and jars  
\- Closures and caps  
\- Product groupings (minimal, curated)  
\- Detail shots (glass texture, cap finishes)

\#\#\# Lighting  
\- \*\*Style:\*\* Cinematic, dramatic lighting with soft shadows  
\- \*\*Effect:\*\* "Chiaroscuro" effect (strong contrast between light and dark)  
\- \*\*Goal:\*\* Highlights the curvature and clarity of the glass

\#\#\# Composition  
\- \*\*Style:\*\* Minimalist  
\- \*\*Backgrounds:\*\* Clean surfaces (concrete, marble, linen, matte stone)  
\- \*\*Key Element:\*\* Negative space is crucial  
\- \*\*Focus:\*\* Product as hero, no clutter

\#\#\# Mood  
\- Serene, expensive, artisanal  
\- Calm confidence, not flashy  
\- Timeless, not trendy

\# Brand Voice & Vocabulary

\#\# Brand Voice: Professional, Warm, Knowledgeable

\*\*Tone Attributes:\*\*  
\- \*\*Sophisticated\*\* but not pretentious  
\- \*\*Warm\*\* but not casual  
\- \*\*Knowledgeable\*\* but not condescending  
\- \*\*Confident\*\* but not arrogant  
\- \*\*Solution-oriented\*\* but not pushy

\*\*Writing Style:\*\*  
\- Clear, concise, professional  
\- Use industry terminology correctly (shows expertise)  
\- Avoid jargon when speaking to new clients  
\- Be specific with details (MOQs, lead times, specs)  
\- Show, don't tell (specific details prove quality)

\#\# Words We Love  
\- Curated, sourced, premium, vessel, collection  
\- Quality, precision, finish, design  
\- Collaborate, partnership, vision  
\- Distinctive, refined, timeless  
\- Custom, tailored, flexible  
\- Accessible, competitive, value  
\- Muted luxury, understated, sophisticated  
\- Artisan, craftsmanship (when referring to our sources)  
\- Sustainable, eco-friendly, circular

\#\# Words We Avoid  
\- Cheap, budget, discount  
\- Middleman, reseller, distributor (use "supplier" or "partner")  
\- Mass-produced, generic, basic  
\- Manufacturer, factory, we make (unless specifically about custom molds through partners)  
\- "Luxury" as a standalone adjective (show it, don't say it)  
\- Superlatives without proof ("best," "finest," "ultimate")  
\- Specific manufacturing claims ("we blow," "we mold," "our facility")  
\- Trendy, modern (unless specifically relevant)  
\- Flashy, bold, statement (we're muted luxury)

\#\# Industry Terminology We Use Correctly  
\- Mold tooling, zamak caps, glass specifications  
\- Lead time, MOQ (minimum order quantity), stock availability  
\- Frosting, etching, hot stamping, screen printing  
\- Neck finish (e.g., 18/415 threading)  
\- Pantone color matching, custom tinting  
\- Sprayer, pump, crimp, closure  
\- Sourcing, catalog, inventory  
\- Roll-on, dropper, fine mist, atomizer  
\- Amber glass (UV protection), clear glass, frosted glass

\#\# Forbidden Phrases  
Never use these phrases:  
\- "We manufacture bottles" ❌  
\- "Our factory" ❌  
\- "Our glassmakers" ❌  
\- "We blow glass" ❌  
\- "We mold bottles" ❌  
\- "Made in our facility" ❌

\#\# Approved Phrases  
Always use these instead:  
\- "We source premium bottles" ✅  
\- "Through our global network" ✅  
\- "We work with artisan glassmakers" ✅  
\- "Custom molds through partner facilities" ✅  
\- "Our curated collection" ✅  
\- "We supply" / "We provide" ✅
