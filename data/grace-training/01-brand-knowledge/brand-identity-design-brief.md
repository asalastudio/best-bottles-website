## Brand Strategy Foundation

### Core Positioning
**Brand Essence:**
Best Bottles elevates the art of packaging. We are not a commodity supplier—we are curators of vessels that honor what they hold.

**Brand Archetype:** The Sage meets The Creator
- Wisdom of 20+ years in the industry
- Artisanal craftsmanship meets modern elegance
- Trusted advisor, not pushy salesperson

**Personality Traits:**
1. Quietly Confident — We don't shout; our quality speaks
2. Artisanally Modern — Heritage techniques, contemporary aesthetics
3. Warmly Professional — Expert without being cold
4. Elegantly Practical — Beautiful AND functional
5. Sustainably Minded — Eco-conscious without preaching

### The "Muted Luxury" Philosophy
Luxury that whispers rather than shouts.
- Think Aesop, not Sephora
- Think The Row, not Gucci
- Think Japanese minimalism meets European refinement

### Typography
- **Display / Headlines:** EB Garamond or Cormorant Garamond
- **Body / UI:** Inter, Söhne, or Untitled Sans
- **Accent / Labels:** Custom letterspaced uppercase

## Photography Direction: "Quiet Drama"

### Key Principles
1. **Chiaroscuro Lighting** — Dramatic light and shadow like Old Master paintings
2. **Macro Intimacy** — Close details reveal glass quality
3. **Minimal Staging** — One hero product, nothing competing
4. **Warm Neutrals** — Backgrounds in cream, concrete, marble, linen
5. **Negative Space** — Let the product breathe

### Shot Types Needed
- Hero product shots (individual bottles)
- Collection arrangements (5-7 bottles grouped)
- Lifestyle/context scenes (perfumer's studio)
- Abstract detail shots (light through glass)
- Packaging/unboxing moments
