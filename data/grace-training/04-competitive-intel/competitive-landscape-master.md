## Competitive Positioning

### Market Segments
Best Bottles occupies the **"scaling gap"**—brands generating **$50K–$5M annually** who have outgrown DIY suppliers (Makesy, CandleScience) but aren't ready for enterprise solutions (Berlin Packaging, Cosmopak).

### Unique Market Position
- **Boutique supplier** model vs. commodity approach
- **Beauty/fragrance specialization** vs. generic packaging
- **Small-batch friendly** with no unit minimums
- **System-guaranteed compatibility** vs. customer responsibility
- **Quick turnaround** (24-48 hours) vs. competitor standard lead times
