# Grace MVP: README

## What is Grace?

Grace is Best Bottles' AI concierge—a conversational recommendation engine that helps customers find the perfect K-beauty products for their unique skin type, concerns, and preferences.

Instead of browsing hundreds of products, customers answer a few simple questions, and Grace provides a personalized recommendation with a clear explanation of why that product is right for them.

---

## Grace MVP Capabilities

### 1. Product Compatibility Answers
**Customer asks**: "Can I use this serum with my acne routine?"
**Grace does**: Evaluates product ingredients against customer's stated concerns and existing routine, provides clear answer with rationale.

### 2. Use Case Recommendations
**Customer asks**: "What should I use if I want to brighten my skin?"
**Grace does**: Asks clarifying questions (skin type, budget, routine experience), recommends best-fit product from our catalog.

### 3. Bulk Order Guidance (Professional)
**Customer asks**: "What would you recommend for a makeup artist getting started with K-beauty?"
**Grace does**: Recommends essential products for professional use, suggests bulk ordering where applicable, provides professional pricing guidance.

### 4. Proactive Information
**Grace offers**: Usage instructions, adjustment period expectations, signs of efficacy, when to follow up with another product recommendation.

## How to Use Grace

### For New Customers
1. Customer navigates to product recommendation tool
2. Grace asks 5-7 questions about skin type, concerns, experience level
3. Grace recommends 1-2 products with clear explanation
4. Customer can ask follow-up questions or proceed to cart

### For Existing Customers
1. Customer returns; Grace recognizes them (if logged in)
2. Grace asks about previous products: "How did the Snail Mucin work for you?"
3. Grace recommends next step (supporting product, alternative, or reorder)
4. Customer adds to cart or continues browsing

### For Gift Buyers
1. Customer indicates: "I'm buying a gift"
2. Grace asks about recipient (skin type, concerns, experience level)
3. Grace recommends a product that's appropriate and gift-ready
4. Grace offers gift message and special packaging options

## Grace's Knowledge Base

Grace is trained on:
1. **Product library**: Details on our 50 curated products (ingredients, benefits, ideal customer, alternatives)
2. **Customer segments**: Deep understanding of 5 customer personas and their needs
3. **Skincare science**: Ingredient mechanisms, routine building, product interactions
4. **Brand voice**: How to communicate authentically as Best Bottles
5. **Objection handling**: Common customer concerns and persuasive responses

---

## Known Limitations

### What Grace Can't Do (MVP)
- Make medical claims or diagnose skin conditions
- Recommend products outside our 50-product catalog
- Process bulk/wholesale orders (routed to Abbas directly)
- Handle returns or refunds (escalated to customer support)
- Provide dermatological advice

### What Grace Doesn't Know Yet (Post-Launch)
- Individual customer history and repeat patterns
- Real-time inventory status
- Shipping timelines and delivery tracking
- Advanced personalization based on behavioral data

## Getting Started

### For Customers
Go to the Best Bottles website and click "Find My Products" or "Ask Grace". Answer the questions honestly, and Grace will do the rest.

### For Support Team
If a customer asks something Grace can't handle:
1. Acknowledge the question
2. Explain that this needs human expertise
3. Escalate to Abbas or customer support team
4. Document the question so Grace can be trained on it

### For Continuous Improvement
Track:
- Common questions Grace struggles with
- Recommendations that don't lead to purchases
- Feedback from customers who used Grace's recommendations
- New customer types or concerns not represented in current segments

## FAQ

**Q: Is Grace a real person?**
A: No, Grace is an AI powered by Claude. You're talking to a trained conversational model, not a human.

**Q: Why is she called "Grace"?**
A: Grace embodies the grace, elegance, and refinement of K-beauty and Best Bottles' brand. It's also a nod to how Grace helps customers navigate K-beauty with grace.

**Q: Can Grace remember me between visits?**
A: If you're logged into your Best Bottles account, Grace can see your purchase history and tailor recommendations accordingly. If you're browsing without logging in, Grace treats each session as new.

**Q: What if Grace recommends something I don't want?**
A: Tell Grace! Say something like "I'm not interested in that product because..." and Grace will ask follow-up questions to refine the recommendation.

**Q: Is my information secure?**
A: Yes. We don't store sensitive information, and your conversation with Grace is only used to improve recommendations. See our Privacy Policy for details.
